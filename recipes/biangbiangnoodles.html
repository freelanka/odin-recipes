Biang Biang Noodles

Description

Biang Biang Noodles is a type of thick, hand-pulled Chinese noodle that originates from Shaanxi Province in China. It is known for its unique shape, which is wider and flatter than traditional noodles, and for its chewy texture. The noodles are often served in a spicy sauce or stir-fried with vegetables and meat. Biang Biang Noodles are a popular street food and comfort food in China and are widely recognized for their delicious taste and cultural significance.
